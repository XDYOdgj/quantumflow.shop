Gibelle Ombre Grey 3D Textured Shower Curtain

Elevate your bathroom with a fresh and modern touch using the Gibelle Ombre Grey 3D Textured Shower Curtain. Made from high-quality 100% polyester, it delivers a soft, skin-friendly feel while remaining wrinkle-free and easy to maintain. The special 3D embossing adds a unique, elegant texture that mimics the look of a ruffle, bringing depth and visual interest to your space.

This sleek 72x72 curtain is designed for both style and functionality. Its double-layer construction ensures long-lasting durability and superior privacy, keeping water contained during your daily routine. The reinforced header and rust-proof grommets make installation simple and secure, while built-in hooks work with any shower rod, eliminating the need for extra hardware.

Perfect for modern farmhouse bathroom decor or any minimalist space, this curtain combines practicality with a refined aesthetic. It's machine washable in cold water and tumble dryable on low, with no bleach or iron required. For extra protection, you can pair it with a liner, but the curtain itself offers reliable water resistance, making it a beautiful and functional addition to your bathroom.
